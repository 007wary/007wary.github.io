How Savr Protects Your Financial Data

MW
Mwnswrang Wary
Developer · Wary Dev.

Financial data is among the most sensitive information on your phone. Here's exactly how Savr handles it — with complete transparency.

The Architecture

Savr uses a privacy-first, local-first architecture. Here's where each type of data lives:

Your Device (SQLite)
All expenses, budgets, goals and recurring expenses — never leaves your device
Your Google Drive
Automatic backup stored in your personal Drive — only you can access it
Our Servers (Supabase)
Only your name, email and app version — zero financial data

What We Cannot See

We have zero access to your expenses, budgets, spending goals or any financial information. This is not just a policy — it's architecture. The data is physically stored on your device and in your own Google Drive.

"We designed Savr so that even if we wanted to see your expenses, we technically couldn't."

Google Drive Scope

Savr uses the drive.file scope — the most restricted Google Drive permission available. This means Savr can only see and access files that it creates itself. It cannot read any other files in your Google Drive.

Ads and Your Data

Savr displays ads via Google AdMob to stay free. AdMob may use your device identifier for ad personalization, but your financial data is never shared with advertisers. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Our Commitment

Savr will never sell your data, share your financial information, or use your expenses for any purpose other than showing them to you. That's the whole point of building local-first.
